# Lovable Website Builder Prompt

## Prompt for Lovable AI Website Builder

**Copy the content below and paste it into Lovable:**

---

Create a professional business website for a services-based company with the following specifications:

### Brand & Design
- **Color Scheme**: Primary blue (#003366), accent red (#ff0000), white (#ffffff) backgrounds
- **Style**: Professional, modern, clean design
- **Typography**: Clear, readable sans-serif fonts (system fonts preferred)
- **Layout**: Responsive design that works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop

### Pages Required
1. **Homepage (Landing Page)**
   - Hero section with compelling headline and subheading
   - Call-to-action button for "Get Started" or "Contact Us"
   - 6 service cards showcasing: Consulting, Analytics, Technical Support, Digital Solutions, Project Management, Marketing Strategy
   - Contact/inquiry section with form
   - Professional footer with navigation links

2. **About Page**
   - Company mission, vision, and values section (3 cards)
   - Company timeline showing growth milestones
   - Team section with 3 key team members
   - Statistics dashboard (4-5 key metrics)
   - Why Choose Us section

3. **Services Page**
   - Detailed descriptions of each service
   - Service pricing table with tiers (Starter, Professional, Enterprise)
   - "What's Included" section for each service
   - Call-to-action buttons on each service

4. **FAQ Page**
   - 15+ frequently asked questions
   - Organized into 4 categories: General, Pricing & Packages, Service-Related, Getting Started
   - Expandable/accordion-style answers
   - Search functionality if possible

5. **Contact Page**
   - Contact form with fields: Name, Email, Phone, Company, Service Interest, Message
   - Business contact information displayed
   - Office hours
   - Map or service area
   - Multiple ways to contact (email, phone, form)

### Navigation
- Main header with logo and navigation menu
- Links to: Home, About, Services, FAQ, Contact
- Mobile hamburger menu for smaller screens
- Sticky navigation or always visible header

### Key Features
- **Forms**: Functional contact/inquiry forms with validation
- **Responsiveness**: Fully responsive across all devices
- **Performance**: Fast loading, clean code
- **Accessibility**: Alt text on images, semantic HTML
- **CTA**: Clear call-to-action buttons throughout (Contact, Get Started, Learn More)

### Tone & Messaging
- Professional and trustworthy
- Friendly and approachable
- Solution-focused
- Emphasize expertise and customer success
- Include social proof (testimonials if possible)

### Business Details
- **Services Offered**: Consulting, Analytics, Technical Support, Digital Solutions, Project Management, Marketing Strategy
- **Target Market**: Small to medium-sized businesses
- **Main Goal**: Lead generation and service inquiry capture
- **Primary CTA**: Contact form submissions or phone inquiries

### Additional Considerations
- Include footer with: Copyright, Links to Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Cookie Policy
- Professional imagery (use placeholder images if needed)
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Consistent spacing and padding
- Professional button styling with hover effects

---

## Tips for Using This Prompt

1. **Copy the entire prompt** from the dashed lines above
2. **Paste into Lovable** website builder
3. **Refine with specifics** like:
   - Your actual company name
   - Real team member names and bios
   - Specific service descriptions and pricing
   - Your contact email and phone number
   - Real imagery or brand photos

4. **Follow-up prompts** you can use after initial build:
   - "Make the hero section more impactful with a video background"
   - "Add customer testimonials section on the homepage"
   - "Create a blog section for articles and resources"
   - "Add a newsletter signup form in the footer"
   - "Integrate email form submissions to [email service]"

## How Lovable Works

1. Paste the prompt into Lovable
2. Lovable generates a complete website based on your description
3. You can refine it by asking for specific changes
4. Export the code when satisfied
5. Customize with your actual branding, content, and images
