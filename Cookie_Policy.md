# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: March 21, 2026**

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They contain information that the website can read on subsequent visits. Cookies help websites remember information about your visit.

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

- **Enhance User Experience**: Remember your preferences and settings
- **Analyze Traffic**: Understand how users interact with our Website
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
- **Personalization**: Provide relevant content and recommendations
- **Marketing**: Deliver targeted advertisements and track campaign effectiveness
- **Session Management**: Keep you logged in during your session

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### A. Essential/Strictly Necessary Cookies
- **Purpose**: Required for basic website functionality
- **Duration**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Examples**: Authentication, security, session cookies
- **User Option**: Cannot be disabled; required for Website operation

### B. Performance/Analytics Cookies
- **Purpose**: Collect anonymous data about how you use the Website
- **Service**: Google Analytics
- **Duration**: Typically 2 years
- **Data Collected**: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, referral source
- **User Option**: Can be disabled through browser settings

### C. Functional Cookies
- **Purpose**: Remember your preferences and choices
- **Duration**: Up to 1 year
- **Examples**: Language preferences, display preferences, saved information
- **User Option**: Can be disabled but may affect functionality

### D. Targeting/Marketing Cookies
- **Purpose**: Track your interests for personalized advertisements
- **Services**: Google Ads, social media platforms
- **Duration**: Varies (weeks to years)
- **User Option**: Can be managed through cookie banner or browser settings

### E. Social Media Cookies
- **Purpose**: Enable social sharing and tracking on social platforms
- **Services**: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.
- **User Option**: Can be disabled through social platform settings

## 5. How to Control Cookies

### A. Browser Controls
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:

**Chrome:**
1. Click Menu → Settings
2. Go to Privacy and Security → Cookies
3. Choose preferences

**Firefox:**
1. Click Menu → Preferences
2. Go to Privacy & Security
3. Manage cookies

**Safari:**
1. Click Safari → Preferences
2. Go to Privacy tab
3. Manage website data

**Edge:**
1. Click Menu → Settings
2. Go to Privacy → Cookies & site permissions
3. Adjust settings

### B. Cookie Management Tools
- [Your Online Choices](http://www.youronlinechoices.com/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- [Digital Advertising Alliance](http://optout.aboutads.info/)

### C. Do Not Track (DNT)
If your browser supports DNT and you enable it, we will respect your preference.

- **Accepting All**: You consent to all types of cookies

## 8. GDPR Compliance

If you are in the EU, we:

- Obtain explicit consent before placing non-essential cookies
- Provide clear information about our cookie use
- Allow you to easily withdraw consent
- Respect your right to object to certain cookies
- Ensure these practices comply with GDPR Article 7 and ePrivacy Directive

## 9. CCPA Compliance

If you are in California, you have the right to:

- Know what cookies are placed on your device
- Request deletion of cookies
- Opt-out of targeted advertising cookies
- Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

## 10. What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies:

- ✅ **Will work**: You can still visit and view our Website
- ✅ **May work partially**: Some features may be limited
- ❌ **May not work**: Login, shopping cart, personalization

## 11. Security

We use secure HTTPS connections to transmit cookie data. However:

- Cookies are stored on your device (not entirely secure)
- We recommend keeping your device secure
- Use strong passwords for login credentials
- Avoid public WiFi when accessing sensitive accounts
