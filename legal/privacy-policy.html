Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how YourBusiness ("Company," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website (the "Website") and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Website.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, business type
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, feedback provided through contact forms
  • Service Information: Details about services you're interested in
  • Account Information: If you create an account, username, password, preferences

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser & Device Information: Browser type, IP address, device type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, referral source
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to enhance your experience
  • Analytics Data: Collected through Google Analytics and similar tools
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Service Delivery: Responding to inquiries and providing requested services
  • Improvements: Enhancing website functionality and user experience
  • Communication: Sending updates, newsletters, promotional materials (with consent)
  • Analytics: Understanding user behavior and website performance
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities

4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that assist us (hosting, email, analytics)
  • Business Partners: Partners necessary for service delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or government requests
  • Safety: To protect rights, privacy, safety of users and public
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Right to Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@yourbusiness.com

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.
