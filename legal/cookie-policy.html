Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and enhance your browsing experience.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • Enhance User Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze Traffic: Understand how users interact with our Website
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Personalization: Provide relevant content and recommendations
  • Session Management: Keep you logged in during your session

3. Types of Cookies We Use

A. Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. These cannot be disabled.

B. Performance/Analytics Cookies

Collect anonymous data about how you use the Website. Can be disabled through browser settings.

C. Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and choices. Can be disabled but may affect functionality.

D. Marketing Cookies

Track your interests for personalized advertisements. Can be managed through cookie banner or browser settings.

5. How to Control Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block all or third-party cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Note: Blocking cookies may affect website functionality and your overall experience.
