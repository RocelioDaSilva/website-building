# Social Media & Marketing Strategy Guide

## 1. Social Media Platform Selection

### Platform Matrix

| Platform | Best For | Audience | Posting Frequency | ROI |
|----------|----------|----------|-------------------|-----|
| LinkedIn | B2B, professionals | LinkedIn members | 3-5x/week | High |
| Facebook | B2C, general business | 35-65 age | 5-7x/week | Medium |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle | 18-40 age | 1-2x/day | Medium |
| Twitter/X | News, updates | Tech-savvy, real-time | 5-10x/day | Low-Medium |
| TikTok | Creative, younger audience | 13-35 age | 1-3x/day | Medium |
| YouTube | Video content | Broad audience | 1-2x/week | High |

### Social Media Platforms for YourBusiness (B2B Services)

**Recommended:**
1. **LinkedIn** - Primary platform for B2B
2. **Facebook** - General presence + ads
3. **YouTube** - How-to videos, webinars
4. **Twitter/X** - Industry news sharing

**Optional:**
- Instagram (if visual content relevant)
- TikTok (if targeting younger audience)

## 2. LinkedIn Strategy

### LinkedIn Setup

1. **Create Company Page**
   - Go to linkedin.com
   - Click "Create a company page"
   - Fill in complete information
   - Add professional logo
   - Write compelling description
   - Add company website

2. **Create Personal Profile (if you are owner)**
   - Professional photo
   - Detailed headline
   - Complete experience/education
   - Recommendations
   - Skills endorsements

3. **LinkedIn Settings**
   - Enable notifications
   - Set up publishing
   - Configure job postings (if hiring)

### LinkedIn Content Strategy

**Best Performing Content:**
- Industry insights (thought leadership)
- Company updates and milestones
- Employee spotlights
- Industry trends and analysis
- Tips and how-to posts
- Case studies
- Videos (highest engagement)

**LinkedIn Posting Tips:**
- Post 3-5 times per week
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am
- Write meaningful captions (avoid "link only")
- Use images/videos (3x more engagement)
- Ask engaging questions
- Share others' content
- Respond to comments

### LinkedIn Content Ideas

```
Monday: Industry Insight
Tuesday: How-to tip
Wednesday: Company update
Thursday: Success story
Friday: Motivational/culture post

Monthly: Long-form article (LinkedIn article feature)
```

### LinkedIn Advertising (Optional)

**When to Use LinkedIn Ads:**
- B2B services
- High-value offers ($1000+)
- Long sales cycles
- Targeting specific job titles

**Effective LinkedIn Ad Types:**
- Sponsored content
- InMail
- Lead generation forms
- Video ads
- Conversion campaigns

## 3. Facebook Strategy

### Facebook Setup

1. **Create Business Page**
   - Go to facebook.com
   - Click "Create" > "Page"
   - Select "Business or Brand"
   - Fill in page information
   - Add professional image
   - Write compelling "About"
   - Add website and contact info
   - Enable messaging

2. **Configure Settings**
   - Set up automatic responses
   - Configure message templates
   - Enable booking if applicable
   - Set up contact info display

### Facebook Content Strategy

**Best Performing Content:**
- Customer testimonials/reviews
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Educational tips
- Company updates
- Customer stories
- Videos
- Events
- Community posts

**Posting Schedule:**
- Small business: 5-7 posts/week
- Best times: Thursday-Sunday, 1-3pm
- Include mix of promotional and value content

### Facebook Advertising

**Ad Types for Services:**
- Lead generation ads
- Conversion ads
- Video ads
- Carousel ads
- Collection ads

**Campaign Structure:**
1. **Awareness:** Reach new audience
2. **Consideration:** Engage interested people
3. **Conversion:** Drive actions

## 4. YouTube Strategy

### YouTube Setup

1. **Create Business Channel**
   - Use your business name
   - Write compelling description
   - Add professional banner
   - Link to website
   - Create branded intro

2. **Optimize Channel**
   - Complete all sections
   - Add channel art
   - Write detailed description
   - Add links
   - Create playlists

### YouTube Content Ideas

**For Service Businesses:**
- How-to videos (3-5 minutes)
- Service/product demos
- Webinars and tutorials
- Client testimonials
- Industry insights
- Process explanations
- Q&A videos

**YouTube Best Practices:**
- 1-2 videos per week
- Consistent upload schedule
- Optimize titles with keywords
- Write detailed descriptions
- Add timestamps and links
- Create playlists
- Encourage subscriptions
- Respond to comments

### YouTube SEO

- Keyword-rich title
- Detailed description (first 125 characters shown)
- Tags (relevant keywords)
- Custom thumbnail (professional)
- Playlists (group related videos)
- Transcripts (accessibility)

## 5. Twitter/X Strategy

### Twitter Setup

1. **Create Business Account**
   - Professional handle: @yourbusiness
   - Professional photo
   - Compelling bio (160 characters)
   - Website link
   - Location

2. **Optimize Profile**
   - Pin important tweet
   - Create lists (curate content)
   - Enable notifications for VIPs

### Twitter Content Strategy

**Content Types:**
- Industry news/trends (share others)
- Quick tips (what's the one thing?)
- Customer success snippets
- Event updates
- Company news
- Engage in conversations
- Respond to mentions
- Thread discussions

**Posting Strategy:**
- 5-10 tweets per day (or less)
- Share other people's content 80% of time
- Your content 20% of time
- Engage with followers (reply, retweet)
- Participate in relevant conversations

**Best Times to Tweet:**
- During business hours: 9am-5pm
- Higher engagement: Lunch time, end of day

## 6. Email Marketing Strategy

### Email List Building

**Where to Capture Emails:**
1. Website: Contact form, lead magnet
2. Landing pages: Free offers
3. Webinars: Registration
4. Checkbox opt-ins: Blog, downloadables
5. Events: Attendee list

**Best Practices:**
- Clear value proposition
- Easy unsubscribe
- Privacy assurance
- Mobile-optimized forms
- Single opt-in or double opt-in
- Segment by interest

### Email Marketing Types

1. **Welcome Series** (3-4 emails)
   - Email 1: Welcome + thank you
   - Email 2: Set expectations
   - Email 3: First resource
   - Email 4: Call to action

2. **Newsletter** (monthly)
   - Company updates
   - Curated content
   - Tips and insights
   - Call to action

3. **Promotional Email** (occasional)
   - Limited-time offers
   - New service announcements
   - Special deals
   - Clear CTA

4. **Nurture Series** (automated)
   - Follow-up to free offer
   - Educational content
   - Build relationship
   - Eventually sales offer

5. **Re-engagement** (quarterly)
   - "We miss you" campaign
   - Special offer
   - Invite back
   - Sunset list

### Email Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 10am, 1pm, 3pm
- Avoid: Friday evening, weekends

**Design:**
- Mobile-responsive
- 1-column layout
- Clear CTA buttons
- Professional email signature
- Alt text for images

**Content:**
- Personalized subject line
- Hook in first 50 characters
- Scannable content
- 1-2 CTAs maximum
- Value-first approach

### Email Platforms

**Recommended Free/Low-Cost:**
- Mailchimp (free up to 500 contacts)
- ConvertKit ($25/month - creators)
- Brevo (unlimited free up to 300/day)
- Sendinblue (free limited emails)

**Paid (More Features):**
- ActiveCampaign
- HubSpot
- Klaviyo
- GetResponse

## 8. Content Promotion Strategy

### Cross-Promotion

**When you create content:**

1. **Blog post:**
   - Share on LinkedIn
   - Tweet link
   - Email to list
   - Facebook post
   - YouTube description (if video)
   - Reply to relevant questions

2. **Video:**
   - Upload to YouTube
   - Share preview on social
   - Embed on blog
   - Email to list
   - Tweet with link

3. **Webinar:**
   - Promote 2 weeks before
   - Email campaign
   - Social media promotion
   - Podcast/interview mentions
   - Partner promotion

### Organic Amplification

**Tell friends and family:**
- Share personal network
- Ask for shares
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Answer comments quickly
- Thank for engagement

## 9. Community Building

### Engagement Best Practices

1. **Respond to every comment**
   - Within 24 hours
   - Authentic replies
   - Ask follow-up questions
   - Show appreciation

2. **Engage with others' content**
   - Like and comment on industry posts
   - Share others' content
   - Tag relevant people
   - Participate in conversations

3. **Host events**
   - Webinars
   - Q&A sessions
   - Live videos
   - Workshops
   - Virtual meetups

### Building Loyalty

- Recognize loyal followers
- Exclusive content for followers
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Early access to offers
- Community recognition

## 10. Analytics & Measurement

### Key Metrics to Track

**Social Media:**
- Followers/subscribers
- Engagement rate
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- Share of voice

**Email:**
- Open rate (aim for 20%+)
- Click rate (aim for 3%+)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep <0.5%)
- Conversion rate

**Overall:**
- Traffic to website
- Lead generation
- Customer acquisition cost
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Customer lifetime value

### Tools for Tracking

**Social Media Analytics:**
- Individual platform analytics (built-in)
- Hootsuite (all platforms)
- Buffer (all platforms)
- Sprout Social (advanced)

**Email Analytics:**
- Email platform analytics
- Google Analytics (track clicks)
- HubSpot (integrated)

**Overall:**
- Google Analytics
- LinkedIn Analytics
- Facebook Insights
- UTM tracking

### Reporting

**Monthly Reports Include:**
- Follower growth
- Engagement metrics
- Top performing content
- Traffic and conversions
- ROI calculation
- Recommendations for next month
